Favorite Food #1: Chicken Alfredo

What is this food?

Chicken Alfredo is a mixture of bowtie/fettuccine noodles, sliced chicken breasts, and alfredo sauce.
It looks like a pile of mush but it's really good.
It gets cooked in a pot to make it steamy.

Why I like this food


  1. It has a creamy taste
  2. You can twirl the noodles with a fork or if you use bowties, you can pick up more than 1 noodle
  3. It's easier to make

Favorite Food #2: Chicken Burritos

What is this food?

Chicken burritos are mexican red rice, refried beans, sliced chicken breasts, bell peppers, corn, onions and garlic wrapped inside a tortilla.
It seems spicy since there are bell peppers in it.
The cheese makes the inside look creamy.

Why I like this food


  1. It's spicy and delicious.
  2. It feels warm to your fingertips.
  3. The corn and bell peppers give it a sweet taste.

Favorite Food #3: Pepperoni Pizza

What is this food?

A whole dough covered with marinara sauce, pepperoni, and sprinkles of cheese cooked at the right temperature.
It's one of the most popular dishes in the world.
It can also be a meal for lunch.

Why I like this food


  1. It's one of my all time favorites.
  2. It's not too salty and has a rich taste.
  3. It's easier to get.
